    Ed Norton’s Early Life and Career

    Edward Norton was born in Boston, Massachusetts, and raised in Columbia, Maryland. He developed an interest in acting at an early age and pursued his passion by joining the Orenstein’s Columbia School for Theatrical Arts. Norton’s breakthrough came in 1996 when he starred in the film “Primal Fear,” earning him an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

    Major Roles and Accolades

    Norton’s career took off after his debut, and he landed roles in several successful films. Some of his most notable performances include “American History X,” “Fight Club,” and “Birdman.” His roles have earned him multiple award nominations, including three Academy Awards and a Golden Globe Award which he won for his role in “Primal Fear.”

    Ed Norton’s Net Worth Over the Years

    Ed Norton’s net worth has seen a steady increase over the years, thanks to his successful acting career. His net worth was estimated to be around $70 million in 2014, and it has since grown to an estimated $300 million in 2021.     Income from Film Production

    Besides acting, Norton has also ventured into film production. He has produced several films, including “Keeping the Faith” and “The Painted Veil.” His work as a producer has contributed to his overall net worth.
