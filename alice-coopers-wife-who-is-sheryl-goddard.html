    Alice Cooper, revered as the “Godfather of Shock Rock,” has left an indelible mark on the music world with his theatrical performances and influential sound.

    Yet, behind the scenes stands Sheryl Goddard, a former dancer, choreographer, and ballet instructor, who has been his steadfast companion for over four decades. Here’s a glimpse into the woman who has stood by Alice Cooper’s side through fame and fortune.

    Their Serendipitous Meeting

    In 1975, during auditions for Alice Cooper’s “Welcome to My Nightmare” tour, Sheryl Goddard caught the eye of the legendary rock star. Hired as a dancer and stage performer, Goddard’s talent and beauty captivated Cooper, leading to a blossoming romance.

    Enamored by Goddard’s intelligence and charm, Cooper found in her his soulmate, while Goddard admired Cooper’s musical prowess and charisma, cementing their bond as best friends and lovers.

    A Private Union

    On March 20, 1976, Alice Cooper and Sheryl Goddard exchanged vows in a private ceremony in Acapulco, Mexico, choosing to keep their wedding away from public scrutiny. Preferring intimacy over fanfare, the couple celebrated their union in a simple and heartfelt manner, focusing on their love and commitment to each other.

    Building Their Family

    The union of Alice Cooper and Sheryl Goddard brought forth three children: Calico Cooper in 1981, Dash Cooper in 1985, and Sonora Cooper in 1993. Engaged in various facets of the entertainment industry, their children follow in their parents’ footsteps, reflecting the family’s artistic lineage.

    Cooper’s dedication to fatherhood has seen him prioritize family over his touring schedule, cherishing moments spent at home with his wife and children.

    Nurturing Their Bond

    With over 45 years of companionship, Alice Cooper and Sheryl Goddard epitomize enduring love and mutual support. Sharing a passion for music, art, and culture, the couple often immerses themselves in shared interests, attending events and shows together.

    Goddard’s transition from dancing to teaching ballet and managing their family business reflects her resilience and adaptability, earning praise from Cooper for her strength and independence. Likewise, Goddard admires Cooper’s musical talent and generosity, appreciating his unwavering support and inspiration.

    A Lasting Love Story

    Beyond the glare of the spotlight, Sheryl Goddard emerges not merely as Alice Cooper’s wife, but as a remarkable woman in her own right. Their journey, marked by a chance meeting in 1975 and sealed in marriage in 1976, reflects a life of simplicity and devotion in Arizona, where family takes precedence over fame.

    Alice Cooper and Sheryl Goddard embody a partnership built on love, respect, and shared values, standing as a testament to enduring romance in the world of rock and roll.
